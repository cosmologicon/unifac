Nightfall

by Christopher Night

Universe Factory Games

Created in 48 hours for the Bacon Game Jam, June 2013

This is based on Isaac Asimov's famous short story.

In the densest star clusters are worlds constantly bathed in light, whose inhabitants have never seen the night sky. They fear the darkness, and are completely unprepared for the night. For at night, the Stars come out.

Click and rotate the suns and moons to plunge all towers into darkness.
